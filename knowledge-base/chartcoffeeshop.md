# Chart Coffee - Hong Kong Specialty Coffee Roaster

## About Chart Coffee

Chart is a Hong Kong-based specialty coffee company founded by champion baristas. The business focuses on sourcing top-quality, Hong Kong-roasted single-origin beans and making them accessible to the community at affordable prices. Chart Coffee is known for quality specialty coffee roasted locally in Hong Kong.

### What makes Chart Coffee special?
Chart Coffee was founded by award-winning baristas including Wilson Chung (HK Siphon Champion 2019) and features Q Grader certified team members. All coffee is roasted locally in Hong Kong with a focus on quality single-origin beans at accessible prices.

### What coffee does Chart Coffee recommend for beginners?
The Nutty Blend is great for beginners who prefer traditional coffee flavors with notes of chocolate and caramel. For those wanting to explore specialty coffee, the Fruity SOE (Ethiopia) offers an approachable introduction to fruit-forward coffees.

### Does Chart Coffee sell whole beans or ground coffee?
Chart Coffee sells both whole beans and can grind to your preferred method upon request. Drip bags are also available for convenient brewing.
